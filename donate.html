The UniCrew is a group of unicyclists from Bakersfield, California. We would like to represent Bakersfield and Kern County on an international level in professional competiton. UniCon is a global scale competetive unicycling event in which unicyclists from all around the world gather, compete, and learn from one another. In 2016 UniCon XVIII will be held in the Basque region of Spain.
